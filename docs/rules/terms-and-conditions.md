# Terms & Conditions Rules (SYNTHETIC — demo only)

- Displayed as collapsible agreement cards, not a single block of text.
- The chequing account agreement card is always shown, expanded by default, and covers the product terms plus the provincial disclosure matching the address captured in Address info.
- A second, credit card agreement card is shown — collapsed by default — only if the applicant accepted the credit card cross-sell offer earlier in the journey. Non-accepting applicants never see it.
- Each visible card has its own consent checkbox. Continuing requires every visible card's checkbox to be checked, not just one overall checkbox — this is the digital channel's consent-capture mechanism (checkbox + timestamp per agreement), in place of a wet or e-signature.
